Mortgage basics: How does your credit score impact the homebuying experience?

Understanding your credit score’s impact could help you be better prepared for the homebuying process.

When you apply for a home mortgage, in addition to your credit score, a mortgage lender will consider your income, employment history, current monthly debts, assets, rental payment history, the size of the loan and your down payment when evaluating their risk in lending you money. While your credit score isn’t the only factor considered, it’s a significant one.
